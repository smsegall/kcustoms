Process

Each pair of shoes are:

Cleaned and prepped

The shoes are thoroughly prepped from the protective finisher the manufacturer used on the shoe and then cleaned.

They are prepped and cleaned to make sure the shoe is ready to be painted and the paint won't chip or crack due to being painted on top of finisher or dirt.

Painted

Then using the best leather paints or dye the shoe is painted with either a brush or airbrush depending on the desired result.

Depending on the shoe material, leather paints or dyes will be used to ensure the best possible customization.

Finisher

Next, the shoes are re-glazed in a finishing coat.

Reapplying the finsihing coat on the shoes protects them from deteriorating and diminishing in quality and also gives them either a glossy or matte look.

Laces

The last step, is lacing up the new customized shoe.

We can use your original laces when lacing up the shoe or new premium laces depending on the look you desire.

Shipping

The shoes are then shipped out by USPS Priority Mail and will be double boxed to protect your purchase.

USPS Priority Mail will usually take 2 to 3 days to ship after the process is complete and we will provide a tracking number.
